Diversity Scholarship

Many more conferences offer free tickets to incourage people from different background to attend their conferences.

You can apply for free tickets and some conferences also offer traveling grants and accomodation.

It can be worth applying for a diversity scholarship!


Who can apply for the Diversity Scholarship?

You should be a member of an underrepresented group in tech, including but not limited to:
  • Women
  • People of color
  • LGBTQI+
  • People with disabilities
  • People facing economic or social hardships
